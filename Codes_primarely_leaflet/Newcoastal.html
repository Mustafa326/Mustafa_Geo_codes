What does this portal represent ?

Pakistan's coastal hazard monitoring is a comprehensive initiative aimed at assessing and mitigating potential risks along its coastal areas. With a diverse coastline along the Arabian Sea, stretching over 1,000 kilometers, the country faces various hazards such as cyclones, storm surges, and sea-level rise. The monitoring system integrates advanced technologies, satellite imagery, and on-ground observations to track and analyze coastal dynamics.
