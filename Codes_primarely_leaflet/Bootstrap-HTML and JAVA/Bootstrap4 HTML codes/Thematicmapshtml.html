Extreme number of corona cases can be observed for Karachi, Lahore and Quetta region which are the gateway to enter into Pakistan through Arabian Sea, India and Iran respectively.Another point of exception lies in the twin cities case. It can be observed that Islamabad possessed high number of corona virus cases whereas its adjacent Rawalpindi city has lesser numbers. Islamabad possesses a relatively clean and open environment with less population per square kilometers. On the other hand, Rawalpindi is densely populated and highly congested. Especially in marketplaces in city region, Standard Operating Procedures and social distancing practices cannot be made to follow even if it is to be because of narrow streets and overcrowded business areas. Rawalpindi also possess lower literacy percentage and awareness. Still it had lower number of Covid cases than Islamabad.
