In simpler terminology LST is the radiative skin temperature of land surface as measured in the direction of the remote sensor. The LST data of the province Punjab of Pakistan was collected/acquired from NASA appeears(Click here to visit NASA Appeears) It is in the unit Celsius and it was collected from the year 2010 till 2019

In general term population is the term utillized to represent the total inhabitants of a place.The data of the Population density of the province Punjab of pakistan was acquired from Nasa Appeears(Click here to visit NASA Appeears) However the population density values were changed to absolute population by multiplying the population density values with the area of the particular location. The population is represented by an SVG(Scalable Vector Graphics). The population data was collected for the years 2010,2015 and 2019

In general terms precipitation is any form of liquid or solid water particles that fall from the atmosphere and reach the surface of the Earth. For the Gulf Coast area, precipitation includes drizzle, rain, hail, and on rare occasions, snow and sleet. Different seasons and geographic locations see varying amounts of precipitation in amount and intensity.The rew data of the Precipitation(area_averaged total precipitation monthly) of the province Punjab of pakistan was acquired from GIOVANNI(Click here to visit GIOVANNI)

NDVI, short for “normalized differential vegetation index,” is used to determine the state of plants' health.An NDVI value of zero means no green vegetation and close to +1 (0.8 - 0.9) indicates the highest possible density of green leaves.The data of the NDVI was acquired from Nasa Appeears(Click here to visit NASA Appeears)
