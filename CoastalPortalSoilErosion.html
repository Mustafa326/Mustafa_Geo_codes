Soil Erosion Layers

Coastal Soil Erosion 2018

Layer Description:
This data represents the soil erossion of the coastal area of Pakistan for the year 2018 calculated using the RUSLE Modelling. The model quantifies average annual soil loss (A) using five important factors, notably rainfall erosivity (R), soil erodibility (K), slope length and slope steepness (LS), cover management (C), and support practice (P).
