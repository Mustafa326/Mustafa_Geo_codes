Covid-19 Map

Coronavirus or Covid-19 is an infectious disease caused by Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrom coronavirus2 (SARS-Cov-2). This virus was first identified in December 2019 in city Wuhan of the province Hubei of China. The virus is generally transmited through tiny droplets that come out of the human body when a person sneezes or coughs and exhales. This virus spreads through human interaction and hence quarintine is one of the best solution to stop the spread of this virus.

Note for map usage
How to work with timeslider

The timeslider in the map by defult shows the data for 30th June overlaping the data of 24th March and hence you have to manualy move the slider to show the data seperately.The range of the timeslider is user defined, the user can see both of the layers (of march and june) overlaped if he moves one end of slider button/control. However if he moves both sides of the slider control then he can view layers seperately .

Fullscreen

The fullscreen control is right above the navigation controls in the map. Use it to view the map on fullscreen of your device!

Home-Button

The home button is in the navigation controls on the topleft portion of the map right below the fullscreen control. Use it to zoom to the server defined center of the map

search control

The pluggin leaflet search control was used, just click the search icon and start searching.

Bar-chart popup

Click on the bar chart to veiw further information
